## Headline Patterns That Perform

Use these structures as a starting point — always make the specific topic interesting:

- **How [Audience] [Achieves Outcome] Without [Common Obstacle]**
- **The [Audience]'s Guide to [Topic]**
- **Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong — And What to Do Instead**
- **[Number] [Things/Ways/Reasons] [Audience] [Verb] [Topic]**
- **What [Trigger Event] Means for [Audience]**
- **[Topic]: What [Audience] Needs to Know in [Year]**

## Content Intent Framework

| Intent Type | What the Reader Wants | Content Format | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Informational | To learn or understand | Blog post, explainer, FAQ | Subscribe / Read more |
| Commercial | To evaluate options | Comparison, guide, case study | Request demo / Download |
| Transactional | To take action | Landing page, pricing page | Contact / Buy |
| Navigational | To find a specific resource | Resource hub, documentation | Find / Access |
