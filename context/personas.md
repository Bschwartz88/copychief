## Using These Personas

Every piece of content should be clearly optimized for one primary persona, with the secondary persona as an acceptable reader (but not the primary target).

- **Persona 1 (Strategic Buyer)** = default for executive-facing content, thought leadership, and anything that lives at the top of the funnel or on service pages.
- **Persona 2 (Practitioner)** = default for technical content, how-tos, and anything that will be shared within a team.

When the assignment brief specifies a persona, write to that persona's pain points, vocabulary, and trust signals. If no persona is specified, ask before writing.
