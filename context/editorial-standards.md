## The Final Test

Before declaring any piece complete, ask:

1. Does this piece deliver genuine value to the target persona?
2. Does it sound unmistakably like [COMPANY NAME]?
3. Would the target persona trust us more after reading this?
4. Is every claim either cited or clearly framed as our perspective?
5. Does it end with a clear next step for the reader?

If the answer to any of these is "no" or "not sure," revise before delivering.
